Codecember 2019: Patterns

I cannot emphasize enough how much progress this work is in.

This project is heavily inspired by a Japanese book from 1824 called “Book of New Patterns” (新形小紋帳). It has a bunch of cool patterns. I found out about it through a tweet by a stationary store in London(???). Anyway, it's cool and available online. Apparently The British Museum has a copy. I would like to see this copy. And visit said store, which has a very active twitter account.
